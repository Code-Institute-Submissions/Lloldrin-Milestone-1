Our Promise

At Signature we have one passion: Hamburgers. Ever since 2012, it's been our mission to create the best burger possible. We've experimented with different cuts of meat, various buns, many, many sauces and, never lost sight of what the outcome must be. The best burger we can make.

On this journey, we've discovered and started to work with the best local suppliers in Stockholm. We are very proud of the fact that everything in our burgers is sourced locally.

When you come to Signature we promise that the burger you're served is the best one we've made. Every single time.
